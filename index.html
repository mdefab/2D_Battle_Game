Player Guide

Objective: Survive in a fight to the death!

Each fighter has unique health, stamina, attack, and defence levels, as well as unique items available to them, so choose your fighter wisely!

Stamina goes down 10 after each attack and up 10 after each defence. It is unchanged if you choose to pick up an item. Items will be equipped automatically for the next round.

Your randomized attack and defence values are affected by stamina, the lower the stamina, the worse they become. They are also affected by the fighter's attack and defence levels. Items can add to your attack or defence level multipliers.

It's a draw if both players do not survive the round.

Fighter Stats:

Athlete: Health: 100, Stamina: 150, Attack Level: 2, Defence Level: 2

Bruiser: Health: 100, Stamina: 100, Attack Level: 4, Defence Level: 1.5

Tank: Health: 200, Stamina: 100, Attack Level: 1.5, Defence Level: 4
